About

Xiaoting Du is a Lecturer at the School of Computer Science (National Pilot Software Engineering School), Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). Her research lies at the intersection of software engineering and artificial intelligence, focusing on reliability and testing of AI systems, AI for software engineering, and LLM reasoning & code intelligence.

She received her Ph.D. from Beihang University (BUAA) in 2022 and will be a Visiting Scholar at University College London (UCL) from May 2026 to May 2027. She was named an Outstanding Ph.D. Graduate of Beijing.

杜晓婷,北京邮电大学计算机学院(国家示范性软件学院)讲师、硕士生导师。研究方向为软件工程与人工智能交叉领域,聚焦智能软件的可靠性测试、AI 赋能软件工程,以及大模型推理机制与代码智能。2022 年于北京航空航天大学获博士学位,2026.5–2027.5 将赴伦敦大学学院(UCL)访学;曾获北京市优秀博士毕业生。
