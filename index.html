Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. Student with the Depintelligence Software Lab, School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), Beijing, China. My supervisor is Prof. Zheng Zheng

My current research interest focuses on empirical software engineering and software testing, such as using deep learning for mining software repositories (e.g., codes, commits, and bug reports).
