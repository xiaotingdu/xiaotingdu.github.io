Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. Student with the Depintelligence Software Lab, School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), Beijing, China. My supervisor is Prof. Zheng Zheng

My current research interest focuses on interdisciplinary studies in (empirical) software engineering, such as using network science for exploring latent and interesting patterns in the evolution of software systems, using deep learning for mining software repositories (e.g., codes, commits, and bugs).
